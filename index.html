Woodland Estate

Breaffy House Hotel known as ‘Destination Breaffy’ is located 3km outside Castlebar in County Mayo on a 90 acre woodland estate in the West of Ireland on the doorstep of the Wild Atlantic Way.

History

Dominick Andrew Browne built Breaffy House in 1890. The house is a Scottish baronial mansion and is victorian in style. Designed by architect William M. Fawcett from Cambridge. The house has boldly recessed facades, a polygonal corner turret with battlements and pointed roof, a second turret set at an obtuse angle to the facade and stepped gables.
